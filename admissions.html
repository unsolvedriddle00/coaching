Admissions

Start your journey with Adarsh Path

Admission Process

  1. Submit Inquiry: Fill out the admission inquiry form below or contact us directly.
  2. Initial Consultation: Meet with Adarsh Singh to discuss your learning goals and course selection.
  3. Assessment: A brief assessment to understand your current academic level (if required).
  4. Batch Assignment: You'll be assigned to an appropriate batch based on your class and subject.
  5. Fee Payment: Complete the fee payment to confirm your enrollment.
  6. Start Learning: Begin your classes and receive study materials.
